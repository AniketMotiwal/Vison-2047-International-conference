CONFERENCE OBJECTIVES

Strategic Goals for Vision 2047

The conference is structured around six core strategic objectives designed to forge a comprehensive blueprint for Bharat's transformation and global leadership.

1

Forging a century-long national strategy (2025–2125) to keep Bharat the world's youngest, most vibrant, and most unstoppable civilisational force.

2

Awakening the decentralised, cooperative, and district-powered economic architecture to consecrate Bharat as the world's largest and most inclusive economy.

3

Architecting India's full-spectrum, future-proof, civilisational defence and strategic security ecosystem — from Earth to Space — for 2047 and beyond.

4

Consecrating indigenous R&D, IPR, and innovation-driven scientific sovereignty to forge one-third of Bharat's GDP and secure the world's foremost knowledge superpower status by 2047.

5

Scripting Bharat's futuristic foreign policy architecture for a just and multipolar world.

6

Igniting the eternal Shodh Manthan so every Garib, Yuwa, Annadata, and Nari Shakti rises as a sovereign co-creator of a joyful, self-reliant, and eternally vibrant Bharat.

Unified Vision

These six strategic objectives work in concert to create a unified framework for transforming Bharat into humanity's most developed, self-reliant, and visionary nation by 2047.
